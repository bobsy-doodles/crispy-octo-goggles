Mark Rober Youtube Logo

By The MasterBuilder
31 comments
My creation is Mark Rober's iconic Youtube logo.
Containing in total 279 parts.
This creation was difficult in many ways because of its angles and low connection.
This creation is a white light bulb with with a white letter M in the middle of the light bulb. The creation has a black background that is tiled off, with a white outer lining with a layer of bricks and a layer of plates
I built this creation because I wanted to test my skills end see what I could create.
This build has a hook to hang on a wall.
